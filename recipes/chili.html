Chili

Description

chili is a southern staple used mostly during cold weather and there are contest built around it.

Ingredients

  • Turkey
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Canned Vegetables
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Brown the turkey in olive oil. Add the onion and cook until tender.
  2. Stir in the remaining ingredients.
  3. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for 30 minutes.
